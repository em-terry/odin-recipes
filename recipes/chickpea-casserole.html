Chickpea Casserole

Description

This recipe by From My Bowl is both delicious and easy to make. Filled with healthy plant-based ingredients, this dump-and-bake creamy chickpea casserole whips up in about an hour and is incredibly satisfying. A meal that even the most skeptic of meat-eaters will love!

Ingredients

For the Creamy Sauce:

  • 3/4 cup raw cashews, soaked in water overnight
  • 3 1/2 cups filtered water
  • 1 tablespoon vegan chicken-flavored bouillon (or sub 3 1/2 cups veg broth)
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste

For the Casserole:

  • 8 ounces uncooked pasta
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 ribs celery, sliced
  • 2/3 cup carrot, peeled and diced
  • 2/3 cup frozen peas; no need to thaw
