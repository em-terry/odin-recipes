Vegan Thai Noodle Salad

Description

This Thai Noodle Salad by Sylvia from Feasting At Home is a delicious plant-based meal loaded with healthy veggies and a tasty peanut sauce. Perfect for meal prep and lasts up to 5 days in the fridge!

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 6 ounce dry noodles
  • 4 cups mix of red cabbage and carrots, shredded or grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely sliced
  • 3 scallions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon jalapeno, finely chopped
  • 1/4-1/2 cup roasted, crushed peanuts (optional garnish)

For the Peanut Sauce:

  • 1 teaspoon ginger spice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/3 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup agave or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup toasted sesame oil
  • 1-1 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to directions on package. Drain and chill under cold running water.
  2. While noodles are cooking, blend the peanut sauce ingredients together using a blender until smooth.
  3. Place shredded veggies, bell pepper, scallions, cilantro and jalape√±o into a serving bowl. Toss. Add the cold noodles to the serving bowl and toss again. Pour some peanut sauce over top and toss well to combine- no need to use all, just use enough to coat well. (Save the extra sauce for any leftover salad-as it can soak up the sauce overnight.)
  4. Adjust the salt (to your liking), add chili flakes if you want, or more lime, and serve, garnishing with roasted peanuts and a lime wedge.
